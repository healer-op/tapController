## 🎮 The Ultimate Virtual Gamepad
TapController bridges the gap between your mobile device and your PC gaming experience. Whether you're playing racing sims, platformers, or party games, TapController provides a seamless, responsive, and customizable control interface without the need for expensive hardware.

### ✨ Key Features

*   **⚡ Ultra-Low Latency:** Optimized WebSocket communication ensures your inputs are registered instantly.
*   **📐 Precision Gyroscope:** Use your phone's tilt sensors for smooth steering or aiming, powered by advanced gravity-vector math.
*   **🏎️ Game-Specific Layouts:** Switch between specialized layouts for games like **Forza Horizon** and **It Takes Two** on the fly.
*   **🖐️ Multi-Touch Support:** Advanced pointer tracking allows for complex simultaneous button and joystick inputs.
*   **🌐 Play Anywhere:** Connect via your local Wi-Fi for the best speed, or use the built-in **Internet Tunnel** to play with friends anywhere in the world.
*   **💎 Glassmorphism UI:** A beautiful, modern interface on both your PC and your phone.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1.  **Launch the Desktop App:** Open TapController on your PC.
2.  **Scan & Connect:** Point your phone camera at the QR code displayed on your screen.
3.  **Start Playing:** Your phone instantly becomes a virtual Xbox-style controller recognized by all your favorite games.
